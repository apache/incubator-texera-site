The **dkNET-AI · Apache Texera Agent Hackathon** brought together researchers, students, and data enthusiasts at UC Irvine from **May 14–18, 2026** for an intensive four-day sprint focused on human-AI collaborative data science.

Hosted by the NIH dkNET project and the Apache Texera team, the event challenged participants to build intuitive, visual workflows in which advanced AI agents co-author data pipelines alongside humans.

### By the numbers

- **23** active participants
- **46** hours of non-stop hacking
- **35** total project submissions (33 accepted for formal evaluation)
