<p style="font-family: Georgia,'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 21px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 0 0 20px;">We are thrilled to celebrate the successful conclusion of the recent dkNET-AI &middot; Apache Texera Agent Hackathon! Brought together by the NIH dkNET project and hosted by the Apache Texera team, this intensive, in-person event challenged participants to build the future of human-AI collaborative data science.</p>
<p style="font-family: Georgia,'Times New Roman',serif; font-size: 21px; line-height: 1.5; margin: 0 0 20px;">Over a dynamic four-day sprint from May 14 to May 18, 2026, at UC Irvine, the event united researchers, students, and data enthusiasts to build intuitive, visual workflows where advanced AI agents co-author data pipelines alongside humans.</p>
